About

Hey there, I'm Adam.

I believe UX design is about improving lives; It’s breaking down the barriers that keep people from progress, peace, and joy:

  • Progress, because UX design cares about people, their goals, and providing what they need to accomplish those goals.
  • Peace, because UX design makes people feel safe by safeguarding them from errors and communicating in honest, easy-to-understand language.
  • Joy, because good UX design makes people feel capable, intelligent, and strong by creating designs that are easy to understand and enjoyable to use!
